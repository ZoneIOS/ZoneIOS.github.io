An introduction to Splatoon

Splatoon is a diverse 3rd person shooter game, produced by Nintendo in 2015. A game revolving around the idea of the "Inklings", beings which can transform between squid and humanoid form. The game has two modes, single player campaign and 4v4 PvP.

About the game

Player vs. Player

Splatoons multiplayer consists of 4v4 action. The main objective is to cover the most area space possible in your teams colour of ink. You can kill enemy players, however the main focus is to cover as much ground as possibe in your colour. Howver, killing enemies is not unnessecary, as it rewards you by revoming them from the battlefield for a short time, allowing you time to ink more ground for your team. Games are 2 minutes long and are determined by how much of one teams colour is on the map.

The single player campaign is very different to the multiplayer action. The singleplayer mode revolves areound the story, and it is based on the fact that the "Octarians", a species similair to the inklings are coming back for an turf war. The scene is set in a time long after the extinction of humans, and the "Inklings" must rescue the zapfish, which is Inkopolis's source of power.

Weapons

Splatoon has 3 different kinds of weapons. Main weapons, sub weapons and special weapons. There are 5 different types of main weapons: Shooters, chargers, rollers, sloshers and splatlings. There are many different kinds of sub and special weapons, each with unique properties. Main weapons are your'e main source of damage and covering ground. Sub weapons cost more ink but have special properties and benefit your team. Special weapons charge up based on the amount of ink you have laid on the battlefield. They are deadly and can lead to advantages.

Splatfest's

Splatfest's occur every once in a while. They appear as an event and player choose to vote for which team they want to win. Normally the subjects chosen are opposite of each other, such as cats and dogs, science and art or pirates and ninjas. If you compete in the splatfest you will obatin a limited edition shirt with subject you are supporting on it.

Accessories

In Splatoon "Inklings" find that the most important thing is to "Stay fresh". This means that they have a constant need to acquire new gear. The entire economy of the Inkopolis now revolves around selling and buying clothes and shoes. However, buying gear is not just for cosmetic purposes, as they have many properties which allow them to benefit your character, such as shortening your respawn time, increasing your speed or even making you use less ink!

Maps

Splatoon has a wide range of maps to offer to players. The maps are mirrored, so that there would be no clear advantage from one side or the other. Maps change every 2 hours, and they have 2 maps in the map rotation at a time. There are 12 maps in total, so players can always have a diverse multiplayer map experience.
